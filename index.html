About David

Finding the Cause,
Not Just the Symptom

Dr David Silver PhD is a Consultant Physiotherapist with over two decades of experience helping patients move better, feel stronger, and live more actively. Based at The Forge Clinic in Richmond and at Cobham Sports Association, David works with patients across a broad spectrum — from everyday musculoskeletal pain to elite sporting injuries.

David is renowned for looking beyond the presenting complaint to find and address the root causes of pain and dysfunction. Using detailed movement screening and a host of clinical tools, he builds a clear, personalised picture of why an issue has arisen — creating lasting change rather than short-term relief.

Working with David is often a journey of self-discovery, leaving patients empowered with the knowledge and skills to protect their long-term health. He also leads the sports medicine team at Cobham Sports Association and has served as Head Medic at Cobham Rugby since 2009.

Beyond clinical practice, David is a respected academic. His PhD at St Mary's University, Twickenham focused on concussion assessment and management in youth community rugby — a field in which he is now a leading voice.

"His reputation has been built on evidence-based practice, building lasting relationships and inspiring patients towards more fulfilled, active lives."

What I Treat

Areas of Expertise

🏃
Sports & Musculoskeletal Injuries
Comprehensive assessment and rehabilitation for sports injuries of all kinds, from acute sprains to chronic overuse conditions, with a particular focus on lower limb pathology.
🧠
Concussion Management
A leading academic researcher in concussion, David offers specialist long-term concussion symptom resolution, graded return-to-sport programmes, and education for players, parents, and coaches.
🔄
Movement Re-Education & Gait Analysis
Detailed movement and gait analysis to identify biomechanical faults driving injury and pain. David designs targeted programmes to retrain movement patterns from the ground up.
🦵
Back & Neck Pain
Expert treatment of chronic and acute spinal conditions using a combination of hands-on therapy, exercise prescription, and patient education to deliver sustained relief.
🏥
Post-Operative Rehabilitation
Structured pre- and post-operative rehabilitation programmes to optimise surgical outcomes, reduce recovery time, and restore full function safely and effectively.
👧
Paediatric & Running Injuries
Specialist care for young athletes and runners, including growth-related conditions, training load management, and injury-prevention strategies tailored to youth sport.
