About David

A Career in Movement.

I truly love what I do. I have been blessed with a passion for movement and learning, and the skills to help people thrive. I get no greater joy than guiding people back to the places and people that make their lives fulfilling. It's not a job, it's a vocation.

Over two decades in practice many things have evolved, but one thing has stayed constant — clients visit me for clarity. A movement pattern un-spotted. A root cause nobody tested. A head injury that wasn't framed properly. Finding what others overlook is the thread that runs through every part of my work — clinical, corporate, medico-legal, and research.

Supporting the special people around me is my guiding light. From my wonderful family to my friends and clients (the same thing!), my goal is to uplift them, as they uplift me. We are always stronger together.

One
Listen
No problem is solved without full understanding. This takes a deeper level of relationship than most clinicians seek or reach. When your journey is appreciated, truly individualised solutions fall into place.
Two
Explain
Someone who understands their body recovers faster. Complex biomechanics, head-injury physiology, return-to-play protocols — explained and documented in plain terms, every time.
Three
Empowerment over Dependency
Gifting the knowledge, tools and confidence to manage and thrive. Good rehabilitation always ends with independence.
